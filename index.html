Background : Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, a lot of restaurants were restricted to only take-out delivery and aren't making enough money to offset expenses.

Solution: Restaurant to Hospital food delivery app that allows hospitals to custom-order food in bulk from restaurants open during Covid-19 pandemic.

Background : A lot of young investors aren't fully educated about the risk of investing in the stock market and do know the risks involved. For example, a 20 year old man took his own life after seeing an unrealized loss of $730,000 on his Robinhood account.

Solution: An app that allows beginner investors to level up as they acquire knowledge about the markets and also allow them to unlock certain features only allowed for more experience investors.

Projects

Background : According to Communication Services for the Death (CSD), 98% of deaf people don't recieve education in sign language and 72% of families don't sign with their deaf children. There needs to be a solution to bridge teachers, parents and ASL students.

Solution: ASL Robot allows users to translate ASL hand movements with the onboard phone camera and voice recording into ASL signs. The backbone of the app uses pattern recoignition by machine learning to understand ASL and users are able to rate the accuracy of the translations.
